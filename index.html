What is
Doodle Ape Planet?

Doodle Ape Planet is an NFT collection that consists of 7.979 adorable apes on the Ethereum Blockchain. The focus is completely on our community. The creator and the artist of our collection blends his knowledge on NFT communities and his art, which results in this collection of adorable apes! We got our inspiration from Doodles and Prime Ape Planet, to which we are sending our best regards. We are certainly not affiliated to neither of these collections; however, we cannot do anything but to praise their success!
